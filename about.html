Wildlife

The area around Mvuu abounds with wildlife. From elephants in camp, predators roaming the area to superb birding and butterflies. An adventure is around every corner.


The Zambezi River

Situated on a pristine stretch of the Zambezi river with a backdrop of the Zambezi escarpment, a view over Mana pools and spectacular sunsets and sunrises. The river doesn’t get much better than this.
